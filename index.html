Biography

Below is about life & musical journey

Amo Mabelane is known as Amplekz who grew up in Diepkloof Extension, Soweto. He started to take an interest in music at an early age and is progressing gradually in his 20s. He is an artist, instrumentalist, music producer, and entrepreneur. Amp is locally known for composing instrumentals such as Hip Hop, R&B, Electro, playing the piano and working as a producer. With progressing sound, Amp is still continuing to experiment with and cross over into various musical sounds and genres.

Amp saw the genuinely enthusiastic reaction to make beats on a home-based studio called FL Studio introduced and showed by his elderly mates in his community. Although experiencing how to compose beats was his thing Amplekz soon understood that he had to learn how to play the piano to shape his playing skills effectively. The activities that Amp is currently involved with are collaborations with producers and beat makers.

While expanding his horizon by traveling international territories. He was learning about the diverse music cultural by trying to innovate new sounds in the fast-paced music industry. The year 2019 he got to work on his single titled Smooth at Tangerine Studios. His second single he had released was Fantastic who he co-produced with 6INCH. He runs a show named: ‘The Amplified Show’ which is his DJ mix via mixcloud.

With Ampz intentions to release an EP, which he is currently working on project named HYPE.
He plans to release it in the year 2024.
